Share Your Clips Now!

Twitcher is a clip sharing platform for Twitch streamers who need help getting discovered. Streamers can sign up through the Twitch api and share clips of their own channel. This means you are not competeing with users reposting content that is not their own. Clips are shared in a way that benefits streamers that create quality content.

  • Streamers share clips of their own channel to maintain integerity.
  • Clips are shared publicly by number of likes to reward good content.
  • Get viewers directly to your Twitch stream by sharing your clips.
